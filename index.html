About Me.

Driven by a deep passion for unraveling the intricacies of history and international relations, I embarked on an academic journey that has profoundly shaped my comprehension of the world. My educational pursuit commenced at Miranda House, University of Delhi, where I obtained a Bachelor's degree in History. Eager to expand my understanding, I pursued a Master's degree in Politics and International Relations at Pondicherry University, culminating in the successful defense of my dissertation titled "The Strategic Significance of Gilgit-Baltistan: A Focus on the Sino-Pak Nexus and its Implications for India." Fueled by an insatiable thirst for knowledge and a drive to effect meaningful change, I am currently pursuing a second Master's degree in Conflict Management and Development at the esteemed Malaviya Centre for Peace Research, situated at Banaras Hindu University. This endeavor enables me to delve deeper into comprehending the complexities of conflict and the process of building peace. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am actively engaged as a Senior Media Analyst, a role that allows me to merge my passion for research with practical applications in the real world. I am always on the lookout for opportunities to grow and develop, seeking avenues that can enhance my skills and broaden my intellectual horizons. My areas of interest encompass national security, geo-strategic analysis, peace and security studies, history, and diplomatic studies, all of which continue to captivate my attention and fuel my unrelenting quest for knowledge.
